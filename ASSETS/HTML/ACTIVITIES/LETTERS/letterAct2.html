Letter Recognition: Activity 2

Activity Description:
  • Lesson Type: PG - Parent Guided
  • Estimated Time: 20 min
  • Instructions: Review the alphabet with your student. Click and say the letter. Have your student click and say the letter. Click on the letter and have the student identify the letter. Spell simple three or four letter site words and ask the student to identify the letters in the word (be sure to use clear after each word.).
